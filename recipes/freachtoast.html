French toast

Description

A dish of sliced bread soaked in beaten eggs and often milk or cream then pan fried

ingredients

  • White bread
  • Eggs
  • Milk
  • Vanilla and Cinamon
  • Salt
  • Butter

directions

  1. Gather all ingrdients,
  2. whisk milk, eggs ,vanilla, Cinamon,and salt together in a shallow bowl.
  3. lightly butter a gridle and heater over medium high heat.
  4. dunk bread in the egg mixture,soaking both sides.
  5. transfer to the hot skillet and cook untill golden.
  6. serve hot.
